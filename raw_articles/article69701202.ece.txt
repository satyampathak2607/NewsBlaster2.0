Chief Minister M.K. Stalin is likely to inaugurate the 16th edition of ACMEE 2025 to be held at Chennai Trade Centre in Nandambakkam on June 19. ACMEE 2025 — the country’s premier international machine tools event — will be organised by Ambattur Industrial Estate Manufacturers’ Association from June 19 to 23. A total of 468 companies will take part in the event, showcasing nearly 120 international brands.
