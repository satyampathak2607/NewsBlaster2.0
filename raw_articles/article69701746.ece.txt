K.J. Srinivasa, Joint Secretary and Chief Passport Officer, Ministry of External Affairs, flagged off a mobile passport seva van in the city on Monday. It has been launched to last-mile delivery of passport services for applicants who reside in rural and remote areas. S. Vijayakumar, Regional Passport Officer, Chennai region, who was present at the flagging off ceremony said the van would initially be deployed in Tambaram locality, and gradually in nearby districts.
